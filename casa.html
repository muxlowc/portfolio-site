--- layout: default intro-title: "CASA - Canadian Alliance of Student Associations" intro-key-words: "Print, Branding" intro-text: | The Canadian Alliance of Student Associations is a not-for-profit organization that advocates on behalf of post-secondary students to the federal government.

Because this organization is responsible for generating numerous documents throughout the year for both government officials and post-secondary students, a styleguide was needed in order to aid the report writing process. Therefore, I created a booklet containing guidelines for font choices & hierarchies, layouts, colour choices, and photography / logo uses. ---
