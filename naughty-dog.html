--- layout: default intro-title: "Naughty Dog Rebrand" intro-key-words: "Branding, Web Design" intro-img: naughty-dog-icon.jpg intro-text: | This project started out as a school project: to create a branding video reel for a chosen real-world company. I knew that I wanted to design a video for a gaming company, which brought me to Naughty Dog. After doing some initial research, I felt that their brand was outdated compared to the amazing graphics they produce. So I decided to make it a personal project to rebrand their company. This started with a logo refresh - I did not want to change their existing logo too much, so I toned it down and made it more simplistic. As for the web redesign, I chose to use their colour scheme and high quality game graphics more prominently throughout to really show everyone what they are capable of. ---
